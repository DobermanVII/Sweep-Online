HOW TO PLAY

Sweep rules

Online modesPlay 1v1, three-player free-for-all, or 2v2 with shared team scores.

Fair playThe server validates turns, captures, Sweeps, scoring, and round endings.

DeckPlay with all 52 standard cards plus both jokers.

JokersA joker is wild and captures the entire table pile.

Match the top cardPlay a card matching the exposed top card to capture the entire table pile.

SweepCapturing the entire pile when only one card is on the table scores +2 points.

Round bonusesMost cards +3, 10♦ +2, and 2♣ +1.

WinFirst player to reach the target score wins.
